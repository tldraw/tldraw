Select a shape and drag it to move it. To resize, drag any of the eight handles around the selection. Hold **Shift** while resizing to maintain the aspect ratio.

![Moving and resizing shapes on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/moving-and-resizing.gif)

To rotate, hover just outside a corner handle until the rotation cursor appears, then drag. Hold **Shift** to snap rotation to 15° increments.

## Nudging

With a shape selected, use the arrow keys to nudge it one pixel at a time. Hold **Shift** and press an arrow key to nudge by 10 pixels.

## Rotating with the keyboard

- **⇧ &gt;** — rotate clockwise
- **⇧ &lt;** — rotate counterclockwise
- **⇧ ⌥ &gt;** — rotate clockwise (fine)
- **⇧ ⌥ &lt;** — rotate counterclockwise (fine)

## Flipping

Press **⇧ H** to flip a selected shape horizontally, or **⇧ V** to flip it vertically.

## Resizing with the keyboard

- **⌘ ⌥ ⇧ =** — enlarge the selected shape
- **⌘ ⌥ ⇧ −** — shrink the selected shape

## Repeat shape

Press **⌥ R** to repeat the last shape you drew, placing a new copy at the same size and style.

## Locking shapes

Right-click a shape and select **Lock** to prevent it from being accidentally moved or edited. Locked shapes show a lock icon. Right-click and select **Unlock** to release.
