Publishing creates a permanent public read-only copy of your file at a fixed URL. Visitors can view the published version but cannot make changes. If you keep editing after publishing, your changes won't be visible to visitors until you publish again.

Only the creator of a file can publish, unpublish, or update a published file.

## Publishing a file

Click the **Share** button, then open the **Publish** tab. Toggle on **Publish this file**. Once published, click **Copy link** to share the URL with anyone — no tldraw account required to view it.

![Publishing a file from the Share menu in tldraw](/images/user-guides/publishing.gif)

## Publishing changes

After editing a published file, visitors still see the old version until you push an update. Open **Share** → **Publish** and click **Publish changes**. The published URL now shows your latest version.

## Unpublishing a file

Open **Share** → **Publish** and toggle off **Publish this file**. The published URL will no longer be accessible until you publish again.
