Press **D** to select the draw tool. Click and drag to draw freehand strokes. tldraw smooths your strokes automatically as you draw.

![Drawing freehand strokes on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/freehand-drawing.gif)

Use the style panel to change the stroke color, size, and dash style. The **Draw** dash style gives a natural, pressure-sensitive feel. Switch to **Solid**, **Dashed**, or **Dotted** for more precise strokes.

## Highlight tool

The highlight tool works like a transparent marker. Select it from the toolbar and drag over content to highlight it. Great for annotating over screenshots or diagrams.

## Laser pointer

Press **K** to activate the laser pointer. Drag to draw a temporary glowing trail that fades after a moment — useful for pointing things out during presentations or recordings.

## On mobile

On touch devices, draw with your finger or a stylus. Pinch to zoom while drawing to add detail.
