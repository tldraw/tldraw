tldraw supports multiple pages in a single document. Each page has its own canvas — shapes, frames, and drawings on one page don't affect the others.

![The pages panel in tldraw showing multiple pages](/images/user-guides/pages.gif)

To add a page, open the **Pages** panel (the page icon in the top-left) and click **Add page**. To rename a page, double-click its name in the panel and type a new one. To delete a page, right-click the page name and select **Delete**.

Click any page name in the panel to switch to it. You can drag pages in the list to reorder them.
