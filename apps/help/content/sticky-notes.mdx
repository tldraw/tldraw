Press **N** to select the note tool, then click on the canvas to place a sticky note. Start typing immediately — the note expands as you add text. Press **Escape** or click outside when you're done.

![Creating and editing sticky notes on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/sticky-notes.gif)

Sticky notes have a fixed color and style that you can change from the style panel. You can move, resize, and group them just like any other shape.

## Tips

Press **N** then click and drag to set the size of the note before typing. You can also press **Tab** while editing a note to create a new connected note next to it — useful for quickly building grids of ideas.
