To export your canvas, open the **Menu** (the tldraw logo in the top-left) and choose **Export**. You can export the whole canvas or just your current selection.

![Exporting a canvas as PNG from tldraw](/images/user-guides/exporting.gif)

Available export formats:

- **PNG** — a raster image, good for sharing and embedding
- **SVG** — a vector image, scales to any size without losing quality
- **JSON** — the raw tldraw file format, useful for saving a snapshot or moving content between documents

## Exporting frames

If you have named frames on your canvas, you can export each frame as a separate image. Open the export menu and select an individual frame from the list.

## Copy as image

Right-click any selection and choose **Copy as PNG** or **Copy as SVG** to copy the rendered image straight to your clipboard.
