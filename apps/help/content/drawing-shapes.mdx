Select a shape tool from the toolbar or press a keyboard shortcut: **R** for a rectangle, **O** for an oval. Click and drag on the canvas to draw the shape. Hold **Shift** while dragging to keep the aspect ratio locked (e.g. a perfect square or circle).

![Drawing a rectangle and oval on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/drawing-shapes.gif)

To change the shape type after drawing, select the shape and pick a different style from the shape options panel on the right. You can also switch between dozens of geo shapes — triangles, diamonds, stars, and more — using the shape type picker.

## Changing style

With a shape selected, use the style panel on the right to change the fill, stroke color, opacity, and dash style. Styles apply to the next shape you draw too, so you can set them before you start.
