Press **V** or **Escape** to switch to the select tool. Click a shape to select it. Click an empty area to deselect.

![Selecting shapes on the tldraw canvas with click and marquee](/images/user-guides/selecting-shapes.gif)

To select multiple shapes, hold **Shift** and click each one. Or click and drag on empty canvas to draw a marquee selection box — any shape fully inside the box gets selected.

## Useful shortcuts

- **⌘ A** — select all shapes on the current page
- **Escape** — deselect everything
- **Shift + click** — add or remove a shape from the selection
- **Shift + drag** — add shapes inside the marquee to the selection
- **Tab** — select the next shape on the canvas
- **⌘ ↑ ↓ ← →** — select the nearest shape in that direction

## Selecting inside groups and frames

Click a group or frame to select the whole container. Double-click to enter it and select individual shapes inside. Press **⌘ ⇧ ↑** to exit the container, or click outside.
