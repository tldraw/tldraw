Select two or more shapes. A toolbar appears at the top of the selection with alignment and distribution options. Click an icon to align all selected shapes to the left, right, top, bottom, or center.

![Aligning shapes using the alignment toolbar in tldraw](/images/user-guides/aligning-shapes.gif)

The **distribute** buttons space the shapes evenly — either horizontally or vertically — between the two outermost shapes in the selection.

## Alignment shortcuts

With multiple shapes selected:

- **⌥ W** — align top
- **⌥ S** — align bottom
- **⌥ V** — align vertical center
- **⌥ A** — align left
- **⌥ D** — align right
- **⌥ H** — align horizontal center

## Layer order

Shapes stack on top of each other in the order they were created. To change the order, right-click a shape and choose **Bring to front**, **Bring forward**, **Send backward**, or **Send to back** — or use keyboard shortcuts:

- **]** — bring to front
- **⌥ ]** — bring forward
- **⌥ [** — send backward
- **[** — send to back
