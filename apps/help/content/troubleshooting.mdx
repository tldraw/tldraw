## My old drawing is missing after I created an account

When you sign up, tldraw tries to upload your local drawing to your new account. This can fail if the file is very large or if the page is refreshed during upload.

To recover your drawing:

1. Visit [tldraw.com/lf/tldraw_document_v3](https://www.tldraw.com/lf/tldraw_document_v3) — this loads your locally-stored drawing.
2. Open the **⋯** menu and click **Download file**. Save the `.tldr` file to your computer.
3. Go to [tldraw.com](https://tldraw.com) and sign in to your account.
4. Click your name in the bottom-left corner.
5. Click **Import file…** and select the `.tldr` file you just downloaded.

Your file will appear at the top of your sidebar once the upload completes.
