If you want your shapes to snap to the grid or to each other, go to **Preferences** and toggle on **Always snap**. This helps you align shapes and keep layouts tidy.

![Shapes snapping to the grid and to each other on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/snapping.gif)

You can also hold **Shift** while dragging to temporarily enable snapping without turning it on permanently.
