Select multiple shapes and press **⌘ G** to group them. Grouped shapes move, resize, and copy as one. To ungroup, select the group and press **⌘ ⇧ G**.

![Grouping and ungrouping shapes on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/groups.gif)

Double-click into a group to select and edit individual shapes inside it without ungrouping. Click outside the group when you're done.

Groups can be nested inside other groups, which is useful for building up complex layouts piece by piece.
