Press **⌘ Z** to undo your last action. Press **⌘ ⇧ Z** to redo it. You can undo and redo as many steps as you need — tldraw keeps a full history for your session.

![Undoing and redoing actions on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/undo-redo.gif)

Undo and redo work across all actions: drawing, moving, resizing, deleting, styling, and more.
