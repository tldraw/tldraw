Press **F** to select the frame tool. Click and drag to draw a frame. Frames act as named containers — anything inside the frame boundary moves with the frame when you drag it.

![Creating a frame and adding shapes to it on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/frames.gif)

Double-click a frame's label to rename it. Frames are useful for organizing your canvas into sections, building slide-like layouts, or defining export regions.

## Adding shapes to a frame

Drag any shape onto a frame to nest it inside. Nested shapes move with the frame and are clipped to the frame's boundary when exported. Drag a shape out of the frame to remove it.

## Exporting frames

When you export your canvas, you can choose to export individual named frames as separate images. This makes frames useful for slide decks and multi-section diagrams.
