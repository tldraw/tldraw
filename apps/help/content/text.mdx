Press **T** to select the text tool, then click anywhere on the canvas to start typing. The text box grows as you type. Press **Escape** or click outside to finish.

![Creating a text shape on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/text.gif)

You can also add text directly to most shapes — just double-click any shape to open its label editor. Press **Escape** or click outside when you're done.

## Formatting

Select a text shape or label to change its font, size, alignment, and color in the style panel on the right. tldraw includes several font options including a handwritten style.

While editing text, use these shortcuts to apply rich formatting:

- **⌘ B** — bold
- **⌘ I** — italic
- **⌘ E** — inline code
- **⌘ ⇧ H** — highlight
- **⌘ ⇧ S** — strikethrough
- **⌘ ⇧ K** — link
- **⌘ ⌥ 1–6** — heading levels
- **⌘ ⇧ 7** — ordered list
- **⌘ ⇧ 8** — bulleted list
