To zoom in and out, use the scroll wheel or pinch on a trackpad. You can also press **⌘ =** to zoom in and **⌘ −** to zoom out. Press **⇧ 1** to zoom to fit everything on the canvas, or **⇧ 0** to jump back to 100%.

![Zooming and panning around the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/zooming-and-navigation.gif)

To pan around the canvas, hold **Space** and drag, or use two fingers on a trackpad. The hand tool (**H**) lets you pan freely without accidentally selecting or moving shapes.

## Quick zoom

Press **⇧ Z** to activate quick zoom. Click anywhere on the canvas to zoom in, or click a selected shape to zoom straight to it. Press **⇧ 2** to zoom to fit your current selection.

![Using quick zoom to zoom in on the canvas](/images/user-guides/quick-zoom.gif)

## Eagle vision

Press **Z** then hold **Shift**. The canvas zooms out to show an overview of everything on the page. Drag over the area you want and release — the canvas jumps straight there.

![Using eagle vision to navigate to a specific area of the canvas](/images/user-guides/eagle-vision.gif)

## On mobile

Pinch with two fingers to zoom in and out. Drag with two fingers to pan.
