Drag an image or video file from your computer directly onto the canvas to add it. You can also use the image tool from the toolbar to click and place an image file you select from disk.

![Dragging an image onto the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/images.gif)

Images can be resized, rotated, and cropped like any other shape. To crop an image, double-click it and drag the crop handles.

## Pasting images

Paste an image from your clipboard directly onto the canvas with **⌘ V**. This works with screenshots and images copied from the web.

## Animated GIFs

Animated GIFs play on the canvas when not selected. Select the GIF to pause it.
