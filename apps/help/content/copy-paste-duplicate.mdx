Select shapes and press **⌘ C** to copy and **⌘ V** to paste. Pasted shapes appear slightly offset from the originals so you can see them.

![Copying, pasting, and duplicating shapes on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/copy-paste-duplicate.gif)

Press **⌘ D** to duplicate selected shapes in place. This is faster than copy-paste when you want to stay on the same part of the canvas.

## Cutting shapes

Press **⌘ X** to cut selected shapes. They're removed from the canvas and placed in your clipboard. Press **⌘ V** to paste them elsewhere.

## Pasting from outside tldraw

You can paste images directly from your clipboard (e.g. a screenshot) and they'll appear as image shapes on the canvas. Text pasted from outside tldraw becomes a text shape.
