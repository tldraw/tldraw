Press **A** to select the arrow tool. Click and drag to draw an arrow. Arrows snap to the edges and centers of nearby shapes — hover over a shape until a green binding indicator appears, then release to bind the arrow to it.

![Drawing arrows and connecting shapes on the tldraw canvas](/images/user-guides/arrows.gif)

When an arrow is bound to a shape, it stays connected as you move the shapes around. To unbind, drag the arrow handle away from the shape until the green indicator disappears.

## Line tool

Press **L** to select the line tool. Unlike arrows, lines don't bind to shapes — they're for drawing straight lines and polylines. Click to add additional points; double-click to finish.

## Changing arrow style

Select an arrow and use the style panel to change the line style, arrowhead type, and color. You can set both ends of the arrow independently — an arrowhead on one side, a dot on the other, or no decoration at all.

## Curved arrows

Drag the midpoint handle (the small dot on the middle of the arrow) to make it curve. Double-click the midpoint to reset it to straight.
