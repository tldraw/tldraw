## Problem:

As a time-strapped marketing lead I struggle to rapidly create high quality creative campaigns across a variety of channels. Existing platform and AI tools either feel generic, sloppy, or don’t let me customize and refine my ideas the way I want to be able to.

## Job to be done:

I need to create a new LinkedIn campaign for the tldraw SDK focused on pushing our build vs buy calculator to prospects who have previously engaged with the SDK. I know how to handle all of the campaign creation, audience targeting, etc but creating the actual copy and assets for the campaign is hard.

I want to be able to quickly ideate through a bunch of options, refine them down, get more ideas, refine again, and hopefully finally take the assets to create my campaign.

One of my big concerns is that the tone of voice and the visuals look and feel like the tldraw brand and don’t feel wildly off.

## User Journey:

1. I’ve already defined my brands assets, brand kit, tone of voice, etc into my company settings. My agent also has access to our site imagery
2. I choose what type of campaign I’m creating (platform, asset type, etc) and provide a prompt to describe the campaign. I could optionally provide a reference image?
3. The agent reviews my prompt, reviews the platform I’m building for and the types of assets I need, looks at our existing assets, and then asks me any clarifying quesitons it needs.
4. The agent generates a first batch of 10-20 ideas
    1. Should it generate 10-20 complete ad assets. I think this probably makes the most sense. But could also see phase one being pure ideation and it generates a bunch of copy, a bunch of images, etc.
5. I review and refine. I can annotate, select ones I like, dislike, etc. I can add in additional reference items, select an area of the canvas and then ask to generate a new prompt off of that. I can add info into that additional prompt
6. I get a second updated batch of assets. I can go do a further refinement on that if I want (can go indefinitely). Eventually when I get the final batch I want I can export that set of images and copy.
    1. Dream world would be ad platform specified csv import files
    2. Realistically being able to download images as separate pngs and copy as csv for each copy bit would be perfect
