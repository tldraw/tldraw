An x-ray minimap that reveals crystal formations hidden inside dark stone.

---

Move your cursor to peer through the rock and see glowing crystal clusters beneath. Scroll the mouse wheel to adjust the depth of focus, bringing different crystal layers into view with a parallax effect. Click anywhere to permanently shatter the rock, exposing the crystals underneath.
