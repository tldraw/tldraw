title: Drawing distance rings with arrows
component: ./ExampleComponent.tsx

keywords: [canvas, interactive, arrows, distance, rings]

You click a shape and drag an arrow out to connect it to another object on the canvas. When the arrow snaps to the target, a permanent concentric ring instantly appears around your starting shape. The ring's radius perfectly matches the distance the arrow just crossed. As you connect to more objects over time, the starting shape builds up dense layers of rings like an old tree, providing a permanent visual history of how far away all its connections are.
