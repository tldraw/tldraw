A slime mold fermentation network built from native tldraw shapes.

---

Paint pools of sugar (ellipses) and walls of salt (rectangles) onto the canvas, then click to drop microbe spores. Glowing arrow veins burst outward using slime mold branching logic, hunting for sugar pools. As veins connect nodes, the sugar shapes shift color through a fermentation spectrum and morph into cloud shapes, while the vein network pulses between green tones.

- **Sugar**: Click and drag to stamp golden ellipse pools that attract the slime mold
- **Salt**: Click and drag to stamp grey rectangle barriers that block growth
- **Drop spore**: Click to release a star-shaped spore that starts growing the network
- **Clear**: Delete all shapes and reset the simulation

You can keep adding sugar and salt while the simulation runs, and drop multiple spores to grow from several points. Pan and zoom with scroll wheel or trackpad.
