Click to crack open a dark stone surface and grow glowing crystal formations that interlock into spinning clockwork.

---

Click anywhere on the dark canvas to strike the rock. Cracks radiate outward and glowing crystal seeds emerge, growing into sharp, faceted polygons. When two expanding crystals touch, they sprout gear teeth, mesh together, and begin spinning as interlocking cogs. Drag across the surface to lay mineral veins — trails of smaller crystals that accelerate nearby growth. Over time, the canvas fills with a sprawling, shimmering network of crystal machinery.
